Milk but Wrong

Description

Do you like graham crackers and also milk? Yeah.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz milk
  • 4 or more graham crackers

Steps

  1. Pour milk in cup
  2. Add graham crackers, breaking up to fit in the cup
  3. Agitate graham crackers with fork
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes or until graham crackers become mushy
